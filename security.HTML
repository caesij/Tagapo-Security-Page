Barangay Outpost

Procedure for Filing a
Barangay Report

To initiate the filing of a report, the complainant must first visit the barangay and proceed to the Barangay Peacekeeping Action Team (BPAT) Office.

The BPAT will then issue a formal invitation to the respondent, summoning them for an official mediation session or investigation within the barangay.

Alternatively, complainants may contact the barangay hotline to file an initial report but a walk-in visit to the barangay is still advised.

Note: Accident reports (e.g. vehicle incidents) are directly reported to the police.

Ramir Paje

BPAT Team Supervisor

Ensures the effective and coordinated operation of multiple BPAT teams or specific operational areas, providing oversight, guidance, and support to Team Leaders and members, and reporting directly to the BPAT Chief or Deputy Chief on the overall peace and order situation and BPAT performance within their assigned area.
